FAQs

How do I sign up?

All you have to do is fill out the volunteer form.

Sign up

What happens after I sign up?

We will either send you an email or a WhatsApp text based on your preference with further details about when and where you would be volunteering.

Would I have to come to the orphanage?

Yes, we believe that the children would be more comfortable with actual interaction instead of learning from a screen.

How often can I volunteer?

You can volunteer as often as you like, whatever suits your schedule.

What if the orphanage is very far away from my house?

That's not at all a problem. We're always looking for new orphanages to include into our venture. Just fill out the contact form and let us know where you live so that we can contact an orphanage which is closer to where you live.

Do we provide transport?

Unfortunately, we do not provide transport. You will have to take care of that yourself.

Will someone be there to guide us during the process of teaching?

Yes, someone will be there to assist you during the first time.

Will it cost us any money?

Only for your transport.
