YoungDaffodils

What do we do?

YoungDaffodils is a student-run non-profit organization that aims to produce a fun environment for underprivileged children. We provide the children with opportunities to explore new talents and engage in what we call extracurricular activities. We urge you to be a part of our initiative as well.

I started YoungDaffodils to help underprivileged children develop various talents. They have immense potential. My fellow volunteers and I try to help them in any way we can. We have so much to offer them; so much love and happiness to share. Not only is it beneficial for the children, it is a truly enriching experience and I hope you will be joining us soon.
